# 🤖 Welcome to Sirosri AI

Sirosri is an adaptive AI interviewer designed to make data collection feel like a conversation.

### 🎤 How to Talk to Sirosri
1. **Voice Input:** Click the microphone icon "Record" to speak your answer. Sirosri will listen, transcribe your words, and respond.
2. **Text Input:** You can also type in the chat box at the bottom.
3. **Audio Playback:** Sirosri will speak her questions out loud! Ensure your volume is up.

### 🛠 Modes of Interview
- **Personalized Mode:** Sirosri will be chatty, witty, and react to your answers.
- **Focused Mode:** Sirosri will ask for specific data (like IDs or Emails) directly without fluff.

### 📄 After the Interview
Once finished, you can:
- **Download a PDF report** of your conversation.
- **Export data to CSV.**
- **Generate a Magic Link** to share your specific results with others.
